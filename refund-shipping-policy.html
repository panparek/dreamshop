Refund policy

If you are requesting a refund, please select the original method of payment as the method of refund, then the money will be refunded to your account. If you forget to change your refund method, please contact us for further assistance.

Shipping policy

Global shipping (1-3 days for processing) If your package has not been delivered or tracking information indicates that your package has been delivered but you have not received it, please contact customer service within 45 days of the order date to verify. For other orders, products and logistics related issues, you must contact customer service within 90 days of the order date. In most cases, the shipment will be delivered on the expected date. However, the actual delivery date may be affected by flight conditions, weather and other external factors. Please refer to your shipment tracking information for the exact delivery date.
